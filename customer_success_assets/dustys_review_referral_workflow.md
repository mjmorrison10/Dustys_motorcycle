# Dusty's Review & Referral Workflow

## Goal

Turn completed successful service into reviews, referrals, and repeat visits without annoying customers or asking at the wrong time.

---

## Workflow

### Step 1 — Mark job complete

Log:

- customer name,
- bike,
- service type,
- pickup date,
- customer sentiment,
- unresolved issues yes/no.

---

### Step 2 — Decide review eligibility

Eligible if:

- customer is satisfied,
- no unresolved issue,
- work is complete,
- Dusty would be comfortable asking.

Not eligible if:

- customer complained,
- warranty concern open,
- customer seemed uncertain,
- bike needs follow-up.

---

### Step 3 — Send check-in

```text
Hey [Name], just checking in — how’s the [bike] feeling after pickup?
```

If positive reply:

```text
Glad to hear it. If Dusty treated you right, would you mind leaving a quick Google review? It helps local riders find an honest shop.
[Google Review Link]
```

---

### Step 4 — One reminder only

If no review after 7 days:

```text
Quick reminder — if you still feel good about the work, a Google review would mean a lot. No pressure either way. Appreciate you.
[Google Review Link]
```

Then stop.

---

### Step 5 — Referral ask

For positive reviewers or repeat customers:

```text
If you know a rider who needs a straight-answer mechanic in Orange, send them Dusty’s number: 714-787-9453.

Best first text: year/make/model + what happened + a photo or short video.
```

Optional:

```text
https://dustysmoto.com/guides/refer-a-rider.html
```

---

## Metrics

Track monthly:

- completed jobs,
- check-ins sent,
- positive replies,
- review requests sent,
- reviews received,
- review conversion rate,
- referral asks sent,
- referrals received.
