# Dusty's Motorcycle Service & Repair — Image Prompt & Photo Direction Library

**Purpose:** Provide repeatable visual prompts and real-photo shot direction for Dusty's website, Google Business Profile, Instagram, Facebook, guide pages, and future ads.

**Brand rule:** Real shop photography should be used first whenever possible. AI-generated images may be used for concepting, backgrounds, storyboards, or non-deceptive illustrative content only. Do not create fake customer testimonials, fake mechanics, fake shop scenes that imply they are Dusty's actual work, or fake before/after repairs.

---

## Global Visual Style

- **Mood:** no-BS, garage-real, hands-on, local Orange County rider culture.
- **Palette:** black, white, steel gray, warm shop lights, Dusty's orange accent.
- **Lighting:** practical garage lighting, warm side light, realistic contrast, no glossy dealership polish.
- **Texture:** rubber, chain grease, worn metal, concrete, checkered floor, toolboxes, lift tables.
- **Camera feel:** documentary, editorial garage photography, authentic and slightly gritty.
- **Avoid:** generic showroom glamour, perfect AI bikes, fake logos, unrealistic sparks/fire, over-polished dealership scenes, models posing like stock photography.

---

## Real Photo Shot List

### 1. Hero shop environment

**Shot direction:** Wide horizontal shot of Dusty's actual shop interior with a bike on the lift, tools visible, clean but working garage feel.

- Format: horizontal 16:9
- Best use: website hero, Open Graph, Google Business Profile cover
- Composition: bike on right third, open negative space left for headline
- Lighting: warm shop light, practical overheads, no heavy HDR

### 2. Text a photo / diagnosis moment

**Shot direction:** Close-up of a phone in a rider's hand showing a motorcycle issue photo ready to text, bike wheel/chain/brake blurred in background.

- Format: vertical 4:5 and horizontal 3:2
- Best use: text-first CTA, social posts, email/SMS campaign
- Composition: phone foreground, bike part recognizable behind
- Lighting: soft natural garage doorway light

### 3. Honest estimate / approve before wrenching

**Shot direction:** Mechanic's hands pointing at a worn brake pad, chain, tire, or leak while explaining the issue.

- Format: horizontal 3:2
- Best use: guarantees section, no-upsell promise, GBP post
- Composition: hands and part in focus, tools softly visible
- Lighting: soft side light, shallow depth of field

### 4. Used-bike inspection

**Shot direction:** Detailed inspection of VIN plate, tires, fork seals, brakes, and chain on a used motorcycle.

- Format: square and vertical 4:5
- Best use: pre-purchase guide, inspection service page
- Composition: macro/detail shots with visible inspection context
- Lighting: bright enough to show detail, not stylized too dark

### 5. Ride-ready / Santiago Canyon prep

**Shot direction:** Bike outside the shop or near Orange/Santiago Canyon context, ready for the weekend ride.

- Format: vertical 9:16 for reels, horizontal 16:9 for site
- Best use: ride-ready checklist, social content
- Composition: bike hero angle, road/rider context if available
- Lighting: golden hour or early morning

## Prompt Quality Checklist

Before using a generated image publicly, verify:

- [ ] No fake Dusty's logo or fake readable text.
- [ ] Motorcycle geometry looks physically plausible.
- [ ] Hands/tools/parts are not distorted.
- [ ] The scene does not imply a fake repair or fake customer.
- [ ] It matches the black/orange/steel brand palette.
- [ ] It feels like a real Orange County shop, not a dealership.
- [ ] Real photos are used when authenticity matters.
