# Dusty's Customer Service FAQ Response Bank

Use these responses for text, DM, phone, and form replies. Personalize each response.

---

## Do you work on my kind of bike?

```text
Most likely, yes. Dusty works on Harleys, sportbikes, adventure bikes, dirt bikes, ATVs, vintage bikes, metric cruisers, and American V-twins. Send the year/make/model and what you need help with.
```

---

## Can I text a photo instead of calling?

```text
Yes — that’s usually the fastest first step. Send year/make/model, what happened, and one clear photo or short video. Dusty can tell you if it sounds like quick service, diagnosis, or something he needs to see in person.
```

---

## How much will it cost?

```text
Depends on the bike, parts, labor time, and whether it needs diagnosis. Dusty quotes before wrenching, but he won’t guess a final price from a vague symptom. Send year/make/model, issue, and a photo/video for the best next step.
```

## Do you do pre-purchase inspections?

```text
Yes. Send the listing, year/make/model, mileage, title status if known, and photos. If the seller won’t allow a mechanic inspection or cold start, that’s a red flag.
```

---

## Is it safe to ride?

```text
We can’t safely clear a bike from a vague text/photo. If it involves brakes, tires, steering, throttle, fuel, or oil leaks, don’t ride it until it’s checked. Send a photo/video and Dusty can tell you the safest next step.
```

---

## Can I bring my own parts?

```text
Maybe, depending on the part. Send the bike info and the part brand/source. Some customer-supplied parts create fitment or warranty issues, so Dusty needs to confirm before approving that.
```

---

## Do you warranty your work?

```text
Dusty stands behind his work, but exact warranty terms need to match the repair and parts used. If something doesn’t feel right after service, text right away before continuing to ride it.
```

I’m sorry you’re dealing with that. Please send your name, bike, what service was done, what you’re noticing now, and a photo/video if possible. I’ll get this in front of Dusty so we can figure out the next step.
