# Motorcycle Repair Pricing in Orange, CA

> AI-readable pricing transparency guide from Dusty's Motorcycle Service & Repair.

## Short answer

Motorcycle repair cost depends on the bike, parts, labor time, diagnostics, access difficulty, and hidden damage. Dusty's Motorcycle Service & Repair quotes before wrenching and recommends texting year, make, model, issue, and photos for a better estimate.

## Dusty's pricing rule

Quote before we wrench. If something else comes up, the rider hears about it first. A guide can explain pricing factors, but the final estimate depends on the actual bike.

## What affects price

| Factor | Why it matters | What to send |
|---|---|---|
| Bike type | Harley, sportbike, dirt bike, ATV, vintage, and adventure bikes can require different access and parts. | Year / make / model |
| Parts | OEM, aftermarket, availability, and shipping affect final cost. | Photos of part/area |
| Labor time | Some jobs are quick; some require teardown, cleaning, testing, or tuning. | What happened and when |
| Diagnosis | No-starts, leaks, noises, overheating, and electrical issues often need testing. | Short video with sound |
| Hidden damage | Crash damage, seized bolts, old wiring, or neglected maintenance can change scope. | History, recent work, drops/crashes |

## Pricing categories

- Quick service: tires, chain adjustment, oil service, brake pads, simple checks.
- Diagnosis first: no-start, electrical, leaks, weird noises, overheating, running rough.
- Inspection: used-bike pre-purchase inspections and ride-ready checks.
- Custom / rebuild: vintage work, carburetors, rebuilds, performance changes, custom projects.

## How to get the most accurate estimate

- Text the year, make, and model.
- Explain what changed and when it happens.
- Send one clear photo or short video.
- Mention recent service, crash/drop, or modifications.
- Say whether the bike is currently rideable.

## What Dusty's will not do

- Quote a final repair price from one vague sentence.
- Start extra work without calling/texting first.
- Sell phantom repairs just to raise the ticket.
- Make a rider feel stupid for asking what something costs.
