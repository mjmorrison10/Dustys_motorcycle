# Pre-Purchase Motorcycle Inspection in Orange, CA

> Used-bike buyer protection service page for Dusty's Motorcycle Service & Repair.

## Short answer

Dusty's helps riders evaluate used motorcycles before purchase by checking common red flags: VIN/title, cold start, tires, brakes, fork seals, chain, leaks, crash signs, and likely near-term repair costs.

## Best first text

Send Dusty:

- listing link,
- year / make / model / mileage,
- seller location,
- asking price,
- photos or screenshots,
- when you need to decide.

## What Dusty's looks for

- VIN/title match and seller story.
- Cold start behavior.
- Leaks, smoke, battery/charging concerns.
- Tire age and tread.
- Brake pad/rotor/lever condition.
- Chain and sprocket wear.
- Fork seal and suspension issues.
- Crash/abuse signs.
- What needs work now vs. what can wait.

## Good fit

- Used-bike buyer.
- First-time buyer.
- Vintage/project bike.
- Modified bike.
- No service records.
- Seller story feels unclear.

## Not a fit

- Legal title advice.
- Emergency towing.
- Dealer warranty approval.
- Final repair price without seeing the bike.
