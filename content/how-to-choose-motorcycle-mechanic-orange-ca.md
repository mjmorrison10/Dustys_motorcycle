## What to look for

| Signal | Why it matters | What to ask |
|---|---|---|
| Clear estimates | Prevents surprise bills and unnecessary work. | Do you quote before you wrench? |
| All-makes capability | Some shops are narrow specialists. | Do you work on my year/make/model? |
| Local reviews | Reviews reveal patterns in honesty, communication, pricing, and turnaround. | Do reviews mention the thing I care about? |
| Text/photo intake | Photos and videos help explain leaks, noises, tire wear, chain issues, and crash damage. | Can I text a photo before bringing it in? |
| No-upsell language | Good mechanics explain what is needed now vs. what can wait. | Will you call before doing extra work? |
