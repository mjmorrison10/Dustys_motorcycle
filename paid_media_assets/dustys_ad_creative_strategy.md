# Dusty's Motorcycle Service & Repair — Ad Creative Strategy

**Purpose:** Create launch-ready creative messaging for Google Search, Meta retargeting, and future paid social/search campaigns once tracking and lead handling are ready.

**Core creative promise:** Text a photo. Get a straight answer. Quote before we wrench.

---

## Creative Positioning

Dusty's ads should not sound like a dealership, a chain repair center, or a discount shop.

The ad voice should be:

- direct,
- local,
- no-BS,
- safety-conscious,
- trust-first,
- text-first.

Primary differentiators:

1. Veteran-owned.
2. Orange Traffic Circle / Orange, CA location.
3. Text a photo for the first step.
4. No phantom repairs.
5. Quote before wrenching.
6. All makes and eras.
7. Pre-purchase inspections.

---

## RSA Pinning Guidance

Avoid heavy pinning at first. Let Google test combinations.

If pinning is needed:

- Pin location/intent headline in Position 1 only for exact local campaigns.
- Pin trust/CTA headline in Position 2 only if message match is weak.
- Never pin every headline; it reduces RSA learning.

Example:

| Position | Optional Pin |
|---|---|
| H1 | Motorcycle Repair Orange |
| H2 | Text a Photo for a Quote |
| H3 | No Phantom Repairs |

## Creative Testing Plan

### Test 1 — CTA framing

Hypothesis: “Text a Photo” outperforms “Get a Quote” because it feels easier and less committal.

Variants:

- Text a Photo for a Quote
- Get a Motorcycle Repair Quote
- Ask Dusty What’s Wrong

Success metric:

- SMS click-through rate.

---

### Test 2 — Trust angle

Hypothesis: no-upsell language improves conversion rate among retargeting audiences.

Variants:

- No Phantom Repairs
- Veteran-Owned Shop
- Quote Before We Wrench

Success metric:

- qualified SMS/form lead rate.

---

### Test 3 — Service angle

Hypothesis: pre-purchase inspection ads produce higher-quality leads than broad repair ads.

Variants:

- Used Bike Inspection
- Motorcycle Repair Orange
- Ride-Ready Check

Success metric:

- booked jobs per qualified lead.

## Creative QA Checklist

- [ ] Does the ad match the landing page headline/CTA?
- [ ] Does the ad say “text a photo” or another specific next step?
- [ ] Does it avoid promising an exact price without diagnosis?
- [ ] Does it avoid unsafe “ride it anyway” implications?
- [ ] Does it use local language where relevant?
- [ ] Does it use no-BS Dusty voice, not dealership language?
- [ ] Are extensions/sitelinks relevant to the campaign intent?
- [ ] Are UTM parameters applied?
- [ ] Is the conversion event mapped?
