# Dusty's Paid Social Creative Brief

**Campaign theme:** Text a Photo. Get a Straight Answer.  
**Platforms:** Instagram, Facebook, Meta retargeting.  
**Primary CTA:** Text Dusty a photo.

---

## Creative Concept 1 — Weird Noise Before the Ride?

**Format:** Reel / vertical 9:16  
**Length:** 12–18 seconds  
**Audience:** website visitors and IG/FB engagers

### Visual beats

1. Close-up of chain/brake/tire.
2. Phone in hand near bike.
3. Bike in shop / on lift.
4. CTA frame with phone number.

### Overlay copy

```text
Weird noise before the ride?
Text Dusty a photo.
Year / make / model + what changed.
No phantom repairs.
Quote before we wrench.
```

### Caption

```text
If something feels off, don’t guess.
Text Dusty the year/make/model, what changed, and one photo or short video.

Orange, CA • Veteran-owned • No upsells
```

## Creative Concept 2 — Buying a Used Bike?

## Creative QA Checklist

- [ ] Uses real shop/bike visuals when possible.
- [ ] First 2 seconds have a clear hook.
- [ ] CTA appears before the end.
- [ ] No fake customer or fake proof.
- [ ] No unsafe riding implication.
- [ ] No promise of exact price from photo.
- [ ] Text is readable on mobile.
- [ ] UTM-tagged destination URL.
- [ ] Event tracking is verified.
