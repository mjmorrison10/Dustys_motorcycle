# Dusty's Paid Media Campaign Blueprint

**Use only after tracking, form endpoint, GBP, and lead response process are ready.**

---

## Campaign 1 — Google Search: Motorcycle Repair Orange CA

### Objective

Capture high-intent riders actively looking for a mechanic.

### Geo

Orange, CA + nearby service radius. Start tight; expand only if profitable.

### Ad groups

1. motorcycle repair orange
2. motorcycle mechanic orange
3. motorcycle service orange
4. motorcycle repair near me
5. orange traffic circle motorcycle mechanic

### Landing page

Homepage with UTM:

```text
/?utm_source=google&utm_medium=cpc&utm_campaign=motorcycle_repair_orange&utm_content=text_photo_quote
```

### Primary conversion

- SMS click
- phone click
- form submit after endpoint live

### Ad copy examples

**RSA headlines:**

- Motorcycle Repair Orange CA
- Text a Photo for a Quote
- Veteran-Owned Moto Shop
- No Phantom Repairs
- Quote Before We Wrench
- Near Orange Traffic Circle
- Harleys, Sportbikes, Dirt Bikes
- Honest Motorcycle Mechanic

**Descriptions:**

- Text year/make/model, issue, and a photo. Dusty gives a straight answer before wrenching.
- Veteran-owned motorcycle repair in Orange, CA. No upsells. No phone tag. All makes and eras.

## Campaign 3 — Meta Retargeting: Guide Visitors

### Objective

Bring back visitors who viewed guides but did not text/call.

### Audience

- website visitors 30 days,
- guide visitors 90 days,
- Instagram/Facebook engagers 365 days.

### Creative

Real shop photo or repair detail.

### Copy

```text
Still not sure what’s wrong with the bike?
Text Dusty a photo with the year/make/model and what changed.
No phantom repairs. No upsells. Quote before we wrench.
```

## Campaign 4 — Meta Retargeting: Used-Bike Buyers

### Objective

Convert pre-purchase checklist visitors into inspection leads.

### Creative

VIN / tire / fork seal / cold start checklist visual.

### Copy

```text
Buying a used bike?
Don’t inherit someone else’s problem.
Text Dusty the listing and photos before you hand over cash.
```

---

## Weekly Optimization Checklist

- Review search terms.
- Add negatives.
- Compare SMS vs call vs form conversion quality.
- Check day/time lead quality.
- Review geography report.
- Pause low-intent terms.
- Track booked jobs, not just clicks.
- Ask every lead: “How did you find us?”
