# Dusty's Motorcycle Service & Repair — Growth Experiment Backlog

**North Star Metric:** qualified text/form leads per month that include year/make/model + issue + photo/video.

**Growth model:** local search + social proof + useful guides + text-first conversion + reviews/referrals.

---

## Experiment Prioritization Framework

Score each experiment 1–5:

- **Impact:** expected lead/revenue impact.
- **Confidence:** how likely it is to work based on evidence.
- **Ease:** how fast/cheap it is to run.

ICE Score = Impact × Confidence × Ease.

## Current Recommended Sprint

1. Connect GA4/GTM or at least a simple event logging plan.

### Week 2

1. Launch referral share page.

1. Post 3 “Text a Photo” social pieces.
2. Publish 2 GBP posts using existing guide content.

2. Keep winners, kill weak experiments.
