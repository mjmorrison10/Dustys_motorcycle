# SOP-02 — Safety Concern Escalation

## Goal

Prevent unsafe remote advice and get safety-critical issues in front of Dusty quickly.

## Safety-Critical Triggers

- brakes,
- tires,
- steering,
- throttle,
- fuel leak,
- oil leak,
- wobble/headshake,
- worsening noise,
- smoke/overheating,
- crash/drop damage.

## Required Response

```text
I don’t want to clear that as safe by text. If it involves brakes, tires, steering, throttle, fuel, or oil leak, don’t ride it until it’s checked. Send a photo/video and Dusty will tell you the safest next step.
```

## Escalation

- Tag as `safety_concern`.
- Notify Dusty.
- Do not quote final repair price.
- Do not say “safe to ride.”
- Log next action and due time.

## Done When

- Customer has safety boundary.
- Dusty has issue details.
- Next step is documented.
