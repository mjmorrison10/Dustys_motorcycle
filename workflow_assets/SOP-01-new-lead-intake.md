# SOP-01 — New Lead Intake

## Goal

Turn every inbound text/call/form/DM into a classified lead with a clear next step.

## Trigger

Any new inquiry from SMS, phone, website form, social DM, referral, guide page, Yelp, Google Business Profile, or walk-in.

## Steps

1. Acknowledge the lead.
2. Ask for year/make/model if missing.
3. Ask what changed or what service is needed.
4. Ask when it happens if symptom-based.
5. Ask if bike is currently rideable.
6. Ask for photo/video when useful.
7. Ask source if unknown: “How did you find us?”
8. Categorize lead.
9. Assign next step.
10. Log in lead tracker.

## Intake Macro

```text
Thanks for reaching out. Send year/make/model, what changed, and one clear photo or short video if you can. We’ll tell you if it sounds like quick service, diagnosis, or something Dusty needs to see in person.
```

## Lead Categories

- quick_service
- diagnostic_needed
- safety_concern
- pre_purchase
- custom_vintage
- pricing_question
- not_a_fit

## Done When

- source is logged,
- bike is logged,
- issue is logged,
- next step is assigned,
- follow-up date exists if customer has not booked.
