# Dusty's Motorcycle Service & Repair — Short-Video Editing Workflow

**Purpose:** Turn raw shop clips into clean, watchable Reels/Shorts/TikToks quickly and consistently.

**Primary editing tool recommendation:** CapCut Pro for daily output. Use Premiere/DaVinci only when heavier color/audio work is needed.

---

## Editing Philosophy

Dusty's videos should feel like real garage proof, not over-edited influencer content.

**Rule:** every frame must either show the problem, explain the risk, prove the work, or drive the next step.

Avoid:

- long intros,
- logo animations before the hook,
- overused transitions,
- music louder than explanation,
- unreadable subtitles,
- unsafe riding glamorization.

---

## Standard Edit Template — 15 Seconds

| Time | Edit | Notes |
|---:|---|---|
| 0.0–1.5 | Visual hook | close-up issue: tire, chain, brake, leak, smoke |
| 1.5–4.0 | Problem label | big subtitle: “Don’t ride this” / “Used-bike red flag” |
| 4.0–9.0 | Explanation | 1–2 specific checks or what it means |
| 9.0–12.5 | Safety / next step | “Text year/make/model + photo/video” |
| 12.5–15.0 | CTA | Dusty’s • Orange, CA • 714-787-9453 |

---

## Standard Edit Template — 30 Seconds

| Time | Edit | Notes |
|---:|---|---|
| 0–3 | Hook | show the issue immediately |
| 3–7 | Context | bike / part / symptom |
| 7–15 | Explanation | what to check or what it may mean |
| 15–22 | Boundary | what not to do / when not to ride |
| 22–27 | Solution | text Dusty / bring it in / inspect before buying |
| 27–30 | CTA | phone, Orange CA, no phantom repairs |

---

## CapCut Editing Steps

1. Import raw clip.
2. Trim dead air before the visible problem.
3. Put the strongest close-up first.
4. Add auto-captions if voice is present.
5. Manually correct mechanical terms.
6. Add 2–4 short overlay labels.
7. Keep cuts hard unless a movement transition is already in-camera.
8. Add low-volume BGM only if it does not bury shop sound/voice.
9. Add CTA end card.
10. Export vertical 1080x1920, H.264, 30fps, 10–15 Mbps.

---

## Subtitle Style

### Default subtitle

- white text,
- black shadow or semi-transparent backing,
- orange highlight for key words,
- lower third placement,
- keep away from platform UI.

### Font direction

Use a bold condensed sans-serif when available. Keep it readable, not decorative.

### Subtitle rules

- Max 1–2 lines at a time.
- Each line stays onscreen long enough to read.
- Keywords only get orange treatment when they matter.
- Do not block the bike part being shown.

---

## Audio Mix

If voice/narration exists:

- voice: clear and dominant,
- music: low under voice,
- reduce wind/garage noise lightly,
- avoid robotic over-denoise.

Targets:

- no clipping,
- voice peaks below 0 dB,
- background music low enough that words are always clear.

---

## Color Grade

Dusty default look:

- realistic contrast,
- warm shop light,
- slightly lifted shadows if needed,
- preserve orange accents,
- avoid heavy teal/orange cinematic grading,
- do not crush blacks so parts lose detail.

Workflow:

1. Correct exposure.
2. Correct white balance.
3. Match clips.
4. Add slight contrast/saturation only after correction.

---

## B-Roll Shot Priorities

Capture these whenever possible:

- bike rolling into shop,
- bike on lift,
- tire tread close-up,
- chain/sprocket close-up,
- brake pad/rotor close-up,
- fork seal leak,
- oil/fuel leak,
- tools on bench,
- hands pointing at problem,
- phone texting Dusty.

---

## Export Settings

### Instagram Reels / TikTok / YouTube Shorts

- Aspect: 9:16
- Resolution: 1080x1920
- Frame rate: 30fps unless source is 60fps action
- Codec: H.264
- Bitrate: 10–15 Mbps
- Audio: AAC, 256 kbps

### YouTube horizontal long-form

- Aspect: 16:9
- Resolution: 1920x1080
- Frame rate: match source
- Bitrate: 12–20 Mbps

## Quality Control Checklist

Before posting:

- [ ] Hook appears in first 1–3 seconds.
- [ ] No dead air at start.
- [ ] Captions are readable on mobile.
- [ ] Mechanical terms are spelled correctly.
- [ ] CTA is clear.
- [ ] No license plate/customer info exposed without permission.
- [ ] No unsafe riding recommendation.
- [ ] Audio is clear.
- [ ] Export is 1080x1920 for short-form.
- [ ] No platform watermark from another app.
