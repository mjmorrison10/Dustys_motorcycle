# Dusty's Motorcycle Service & Repair — Video Optimization Playbook

**Purpose:** Turn real shop footage into videos that earn attention, educate riders, and drive text-first leads.

**Primary platforms:** Instagram Reels, YouTube Shorts, TikTok, Facebook Reels.  
**Secondary platform:** YouTube long-form for evergreen repair education.

**Core video promise:** Text a photo. Get a straight answer.

---

## Video Strategy

Dusty's video should feel like a mechanic showing you what matters — not a polished dealership ad.

The highest-value video formats are:

1. quick diagnosis education,
2. ride-ready safety checks,
3. used-bike buyer red flags,
4. no-upsell proof,
5. real shop work before/after,
6. “what to text Dusty” explainers.

---

## Retention Rules

### First 3 seconds

No logos. No intro. No “hey guys.”

Start with:

- the problem,
- the worn part,
- the sound,
- the red flag,
- the bike on the lift,
- the question riders already have.

Examples:

```text
Don’t ride with a brake lever that feels like this.
```

```text
This is why you cold-start a used bike before buying.
```

```text
If your chain looks like this, text a mechanic before the ride.
```

## Short-Form Structure

### 15-second format

| Time | Content |
|---:|---|
| 0–2s | Hook / visual red flag |
| 2–6s | Show the part or issue |
| 6–11s | Explain what it means |
| 11–15s | CTA: text Dusty a photo |

### 30-second format

| Time | Content |
|---:|---|
| 0–3s | Hook |
| 3–8s | Context: bike/part/symptom |
| 8–18s | Diagnosis explanation or checklist |
| 18–25s | What to do / safety boundary |
| 25–30s | CTA |

## Repeatable Video Series

### Series 1 — “Would You Ride This?”

**Goal:** comments, saves, education.

Format:

1. Show tire/brake/chain/leak.
2. Ask: “Would you ride this?”
3. Reveal: “Here’s what I’d check first.”
4. CTA: “Text Dusty a photo if you’re not sure.”

---

### Series 2 — “Before You Buy That Bike”

**Goal:** pre-purchase inspection leads.

Topics:

- cold start,
- VIN/title,
- tire date codes,
- fork seals,
- chain/sprockets,
- crash signs,
- seller red flags.

---

### Series 3 — “What This Means”

**Goal:** make Dusty the no-BS explainer.

Topics:

- spongy brake lever,
- chain tight spots,
- fork seal leak,
- blue smoke,
- dead battery vs charging issue,
- tire cupping.

---

### Series 4 — “Quote Before We Wrench”

**Goal:** trust proof.

Show:

- problem part,
- explanation,
- what can wait,
- what needs work now.
