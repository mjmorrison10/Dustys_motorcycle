Used-bike buyer protection

Pre-Purchase Motorcycle Inspection in Orange, CA

Buying used from Marketplace, Craigslist, a private seller, or a dealer? Dusty helps you avoid inheriting someone else’s expensive problem before you hand over cash.

Dusty's rule: if the seller will not allow a cold start, VIN/title check, or mechanic inspection, treat that as a serious red flag.

What we look for

Paperwork & identity

  • VIN/title match
  • registration status
  • seller story
  • salvage or lien red flags

Mechanical condition

  • cold start behavior
  • leaks and smoke
  • brakes, tires, chain
  • fork seals and suspension

Crash / abuse signs

  • bars, levers, pegs, cases
  • subframe or fork alignment
  • mismatched parts
  • fresh paint or coverups

Real cost to own

  • wear items due now
  • parts likely needed
  • diagnostic concerns
  • what can wait vs. what cannot

Best first text

  • Send the listing link.
  • Send year / make / model / mileage.
  • Send seller location and asking price.
  • Send photos or screenshots of any red flags.
  • Say when you need to decide.

Good fit / not a fit

Good fit

Used bike buyer, first-time buyer, vintage/project bike, modified bike, no service records, or seller story that feels unclear.

Not a fit

If you need legal title advice, emergency towing, dealer warranty approval, or a final repair price without seeing the bike.
