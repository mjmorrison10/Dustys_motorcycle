# Dusty's Motorcycle Service & Repair — Pricing Strategy Playbook

**Purpose:** Give Dusty a margin-protecting pricing framework without publishing unapproved prices.

**Important:** This document is strategic. Any public price, diagnostic fee, package, warranty, or discount must be confirmed by Dusty before use.

---

## Pricing Position

Dusty should not compete as the cheapest motorcycle mechanic in Orange County. The defensible position is:

> fair independent-shop pricing with honest diagnosis, no phantom repairs, quote-before-wrenching, and owner-operated trust.

Competing on cheapest price would undermine the brand and attract the wrong jobs. Competing on transparent process protects trust and margin.

---

## Pricing Principles

1. **Quote before wrenching.** No surprise work.
2. **Diagnosis is valuable.** Problem-solving time should not be treated as free when real testing is required.
3. **Parts and labor should be separated.** Riders should understand what they are paying for.
4. **Rush work should cost more.** Urgent turnaround consumes capacity.
5. **Discounts require a reason.** Referral, review, veteran/community, or package — never random haggling.
6. **Pre-purchase inspections are a strategic entry offer.** They create trust and can prevent expensive buyer mistakes.

---

## Service Packaging Framework

### Quick Service

Examples:
- oil service,
- chain adjustment/lube,
- brake pads,
- tire mount/balance,
- basic safety check.

Pricing approach:
- menu-style where scope is predictable,
- parts separate when appropriate,
- clear labor minimum.

### Diagnostic Work

Examples:
- no-start,
- electrical,
- leaks,
- weird noises,
- overheating,
- running rough.

Pricing approach:
- diagnostic minimum / first block of time,
- quote next step after initial findings,
- avoid unlimited open-ended diagnosis without customer approval.

### Pre-Purchase Inspection

Purpose:
- low-risk entry offer,
- high perceived value,
- strong trust-builder.

Pricing approach:
- fixed starting package if scope is standardized,
- premium tier for deeper inspection / report / test ride if offered.

### Custom / Rebuild / Vintage

Examples:
- carburetors,
- vintage restorations,
- full rebuilds,
- custom projects.

Pricing approach:
- estimate range only after inspection,
- staged approvals,
- deposits for parts or large projects,
- avoid underquoting unknown old-bike problems.

## Discount Policy

Approved discount types:

| Discount | Use Case | Guardrail |
|---|---|---|
| Referral credit | customer refers another rider | fixed credit, one-time use |
| Review thank-you | after completed service if allowed by platform rules | do not buy reviews or require positivity |
| Package savings | bundled maintenance checklist | only if total margin remains healthy |
| Veteran/community | if Dusty chooses | clear eligibility and cap |

Avoid:

- random haggling,
- discounting diagnosis,
- discounting parts below cost,
- permanent coupons,
- “beat any price” positioning.

---

## Metrics to Track

- average ticket,
- gross margin by job type,
- diagnostic approval rate,
- repair approval rate after diagnosis,
- quote acceptance rate,
- parts margin,
- labor hours estimated vs. actual,
- repeat customer rate,
- referral rate,
- discounts given and reason.

## Public Pricing Language

Use:

```text
We quote before we wrench. Final price depends on the bike, parts, labor time, and what we find during diagnosis. Text year/make/model, issue, and a photo for the fastest next step.
```

Avoid:

```text
Cheapest motorcycle repair in Orange County.
```

Avoid:

```text
We can quote any repair by text.
```
