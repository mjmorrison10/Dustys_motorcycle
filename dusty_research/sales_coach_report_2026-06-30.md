## Five Behaviors to Coach

1. Fast acknowledgement.
2. Complete triage.
3. Safety boundary.
4. Quote-before-wrenching.
5. Follow-up discipline.

---

## Weekly Coaching Cadence

Recommended 15-minute weekly lead review:

- inbound leads,
- missed calls/texts,
- response time,
- complete bike info rate,
- photo/video capture rate,
- quote approvals,
- no-shows,
- completed jobs,
- reviews requested.

---

## Role Plays Created

1. Vague noise: “My bike is making a weird noise. How much to fix it?”
2. Safety concern: “My brake lever feels soft but I need to ride tomorrow.”
3. Price shopper: “Another shop said they can do it cheaper.”
4. Used bike buyer: “Seller says it runs great. Should I buy it?”

## Sales Coach Verdict

Dusty’s best sales coaching lever is not a script — it is a weekly habit of reviewing real interactions and improving one behavior at a time. The new coaching assets turn lead handling into a repeatable skill: respond fast, ask better questions, set safe boundaries, quote before wrenching, and follow up.
