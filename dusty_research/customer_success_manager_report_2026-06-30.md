## Executive Summary

Dusty’s customer success opportunity is simple: every successful repair should create more trust after pickup.

Customer success for Dusty’s means making sure riders:

- understand what was done,
- know what to watch next,
- feel the price/work was fair,
- ride away confident,
- return for future service,
- leave a review or refer another rider.

## Customer Success Lifecycle

1. New inquiry.
2. Active job.
3. Pickup / handoff.
4. 2–7 day post-service check-in.
5. Review request if positive.
6. 30–60 day referral ask.
7. 90–180 day service reminder.

---

## Key Rule

Do not ask for a review before confirming the customer is satisfied. If there is an unresolved issue, recover the experience first.

## Customer Success Manager Verdict

Dusty’s strongest retention loop is post-service follow-up. A simple check-in, review ask, referral ask, and reminder cadence can turn completed jobs into local proof, repeat visits, and word-of-mouth growth.
