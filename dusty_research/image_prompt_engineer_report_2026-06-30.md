## Real Photo Shot List Created

The library defines five high-priority real-photo shots:

1. **Hero shop environment**  
   Wide shop interior with bike on lift and headline-safe negative space.

2. **Text a photo / diagnosis moment**  
   Phone in rider’s hand beside bike issue, supporting the text-first CTA.

3. **Honest estimate / approve before wrenching**  
   Mechanic hands pointing at worn part or issue.

4. **Used-bike inspection**  
   VIN, fork seals, tires, brakes, chain, and seller red-flag details.

5. **Ride-ready / Santiago Canyon prep**  
   Bike outside shop or canyon context, ready for a weekend ride.
