## Executive Summary

Dusty should not start paid social with broad cold prospecting.

Paid social should begin as retargeting and trust reinforcement:

1. Bring back website and guide visitors.
2. Convert social engagers into text-first leads.
3. Promote used-bike and ride-ready education.
4. Amplify real shop proof.

The core paid social promise is:

> **Text a photo. Get a straight answer. No phantom repairs. Quote before we wrench.**

## Recommended First Campaigns

### Campaign 1 — Meta Retargeting: Text a Photo

Audience:

- website visitors 30 days,
- IG/FB engagers 365 days.

Goal:

- convert warm visitors to SMS/call/form leads.

### Campaign 2 — Used-Bike Buyers

Audience:

- pre-purchase guide visitors,
- pricing guide visitors,
- social engagers.

Goal:

- drive pre-purchase inspection leads.

### Campaign 3 — Ride-Ready Retargeting

Audience:

- guide visitors,
- site visitors,
- social engagers.

Goal:

- maintenance / safety-check leads.

### Campaign 4 — Engagement Boost Test

Goal:

- identify which organic Reels or shop proof creatives deserve conversion spend.
