## Key Customer Service Rules

1. Acknowledge before solving.
2. Do not guess final price from vague symptoms.
3. Do not clear safety issues remotely.
4. Escalate post-service issues to Dusty quickly.
5. Do not ask for reviews when there is an unresolved concern.
6. Document every escalation and commitment.
