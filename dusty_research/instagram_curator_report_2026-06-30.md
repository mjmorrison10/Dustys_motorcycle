## Instagram Aesthetic

### Core look

- real garage documentary,
- black/orange/steel,
- warm shop light,
- close-up mechanical texture,
- actual bikes and actual shop work.

### Avoid

- glossy dealership ads,
- fake influencer energy,
- generic AI motorcycle glamour,
- fake customers or fake repair proof.

### Grid rhythm

Use a repeating 3-post pattern:

1. **Proof post** — real bike / real repair.
2. **Education post** — checklist, carousel, repair lesson.
3. **Trust post** — review, no-upsell promise, veteran-owned/local proof.
