## Editing Standards

### Hook

- First visual hook in 1–3 seconds.
- No logo intro.
- No “hey guys.”
- No dead air.

### Captions

- Readable on mobile.
- White text with shadow/backing.
- Orange highlight for key words only.
- Lower third placement.
- Do not block the bike part.

### Audio

- Voice must be clear and dominant.
- Background music stays low.
- Use light noise reduction only.
- Avoid underwater AI-denoise artifacts.

### Color

- Realistic shop color.
- Warm but not fake.
- Preserve mechanical detail.
- Avoid overdone cinematic grading.

## Export Defaults

For Reels / TikTok / Shorts:

- 9:16
- 1080x1920
- 30fps
- H.264
- 10–15 Mbps
- AAC audio
