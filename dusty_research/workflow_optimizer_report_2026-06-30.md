## Core Bottlenecks

1. Lead capture exists, but response workflow must be systemized.
2. Form endpoint is not operational.
3. Too many playbooks can overwhelm execution.
4. Reviews and referrals require timing discipline.

---

## Recommended Operating Rhythm

### Daily — 10 minutes

- Check new inquiries.
- Reply with intake macro.
- Log source and bike/issue.
- Flag safety/post-service issues.
- Capture one shop photo/video if possible.

### Twice weekly — 15 minutes

- Post one real repair/education item.
- Post one Google Business Profile update.
- Save content ideas from customer questions.

### Weekly — 20 minutes

- Review lead log.
- Review completed jobs for review eligibility.
- Send review/referral follow-ups.
- Check top conversion events.
- Pick one growth experiment to continue or kill.

---

## Automation Opportunities

- Form → lead log.
- SMS auto-reply.
- Review request reminder.
- Weekly report template.
- Social post batching.
- Lead source capture.

---

## Workflow Optimizer Verdict

Start manual, measure weekly, and automate only the workflows that prove repetitive and valuable. The highest-leverage next step is not another channel — it is disciplined execution of lead intake, follow-up, reviews, and weekly measurement.
