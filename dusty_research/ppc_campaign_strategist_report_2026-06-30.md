## Bidding Strategy

### Phase 1 — Low data

Use manual CPC or Maximize Clicks with CPC cap. Start with exact and phrase match. Review search terms aggressively.

### Phase 2 — Conversion data exists

After 20–30 qualified primary conversions, test Maximize Conversions.

### Phase 3 — Scale

Only scale if qualified lead cost, booked-job rate, and lead response capacity are proven.
