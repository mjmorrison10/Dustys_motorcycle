# Executive Summary: Dusty's Motorcycle Growth System

## 1. SITUATION OVERVIEW

Dusty’s Motorcycle Service & Repair has moved from a single lead-capture website into a full local growth operating system. Across **33 specialist agent passes**, the repo now includes SEO, AEO, CRO, UX, content, social, paid media, sales, analytics, and operational playbooks. The remaining gap is no longer strategy or assets; it is execution: form endpoint, tracking, listing cleanup, owner approvals, and weekly operating cadence.

## 2. KEY FINDINGS

**Finding 1:** The website now supports text-first conversion with hero SMS CTAs, sticky mobile CTA, text-builder prototype, guide CTAs, referral sharing, and `dataLayer` events. **Strategic implication: lead capture is structurally stronger, but GA4/GTM and the real form endpoint are still required to measure and convert reliably.**

**Finding 2:** The content base now includes **5 public guide pages**, **6 Markdown alternatives**, `llms.txt`, `llms-full.txt`, FAQ schema, AI crawler rules, agent permissions, and MCP action declarations. **Strategic implication: Dusty is unusually well-prepared for SEO, AI citation, and agentic search compared with a typical local repair shop.**

**Finding 3:** Marketing operations now include **40 supporting assets/playbooks** across social, Instagram, Reddit, LinkedIn, email, paid search, paid social, video, pricing, sales, growth, intelligence, and analytics. **Strategic implication: the business has moved from ad-hoc marketing to a documented repeatable system.**

**Finding 4:** Owner-side blockers remain concentrated in **14 critical tasks**, including address/hour confirmation, GBP/Birdeye claiming, SMS auto-reply, form endpoint, warranty/pricing approval, and more photos. **Strategic implication: execution risk now sits primarily with business operations, not website strategy.**

## 3. BUSINESS IMPACT

The earlier business case estimated **10–30 qualified leads/month within 6 months**, converting to **4–18 jobs/month** and **$800–$3,600 incremental monthly revenue** at a $200 average ticket. The new system increases the probability of that outcome by adding multiple compounding channels, but paid media should remain paused until tracking, form capture, and response workflows are operational.
